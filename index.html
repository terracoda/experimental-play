Balloons and Static Electricity?

Two interactive experiences with balloons and static electricity

Description

To create the Balloons and Static Electricity simulation as an interactive art installation and to have the audience reflect on and describe their experience.

My Process

Group discussion of the Balloons and Static Electricity sim lead to a discussion around playful language that could be used to describe the balloon behaviour.

Question: How would others describe the behaviour exhibited in the simulation/installation? Could interaction with the real-life experiment prompt ideas for creative playful language and description that would be enlightening for creating an inclusive simulation?

The Live Experience

Prompts?

What do you see, hear and feel?

Other potential vocabulary-based prompts: static electricity, positive, negative, protons, neutrons, attraction, repulsion. Add your words? Guestbook to capture the audience's words.
