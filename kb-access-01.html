Introducing Balloons and Static Electricity

The Play Area contains a woolly sweater on the left, a helium balloon in the middle, and a wall on the right. The charge of each object is represented by an even pattern of red circles with plus-signs and blue circles with minus-signs. Each object starts with equal amounts of positive and negative charges. The Control Panel contains settings to change views, add and remove objects and reset the experiment.
