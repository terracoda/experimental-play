Balloons and Static Electricity

Scene Summary

This simulation contains a Play Area and a Control Panel. The Play Area is a room. It has a balloon, a sweater and a wall. The Control Panel allows you to add a balloon, remove wall and reset entire experiment. Currently, sweater and wall have many pairs of negative and positive charges, the balloon, just a few. Balloon is in middle of Play Area, evenly between sweater and wall. Tab to navigate to next object. Press question mark for keyboard commands and help.‬
