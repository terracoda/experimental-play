Balloons & Static Electricity

A balloon floats evenly between a large woolly sweater and a removable wall. The top of the balloon is level with the sweater just below the shoulder.

Each object shows negative and positive charges. Charges are shown in pairs. Currently, the sweater and the wall have a lot charges, the balloon, just a few.

Below these objects there are controls to add a second balloon, adjust charge views are to reset the entire experiment.

Tab for balloon. Select K or question mark for keyboard help. Select H for list of the heading outline.

Play Area

Contains the sweater, a balloon and the wall.

Sweater

Has a neutral charge with no more positive charges than negative ones.

Balloon

The balloon has no more negative charges than positive ones.

Positioned evenly between sweater and wall, the top of the balloon is level with the sweater just below the shoulder.

Select any Arrow key to grab and drag the balloon. Tab for next item. K or question mark for keyboard help.

Select to conduct experiments with or without the wall.

Control Panel

Charge Settings

Select how you want to see or hear the charges.

Balloon Controls

Select to conduct experiments two balloons or just one.

Select to return balloon(s) initial position and uncharged state.

Resetting the simulation returns the charge of all objects in the Play Area to their original state.
