Scene Summary

A balloon floats in the middle of a Play Area between a woolly sweater and a removable wall. Each object shows negative and positive charges. Charges are shown in pairs. Currently, the sweater and wall have many pairs of charges, the balloon, just a few. Below the Play Area, a Control Panel allows you to add and remove objects, adjust charge views and to reset the entire experiment. Select Tab for next item. Select question mark for keyboard help.

Play Area

Place to experiment with balloons, sweater and wall.

Sweater

The sweater has a neutral charge, no more positive charges than negative ones.

Balloon

The balloon has no more negative charges than positive ones. Currently, positioned at equal distance between sweater and wall. The sweater is to the left and the wall is to the right. The top of the balloon is level with the chest area of the sweater. Select any Arrow key to grab the balloon and start dragging. Select Tab for next item. Select question mark for keyboard help.

Wall

The wall has no more positive charges than negative ones.
