Scene Summary

In the Play Area, there are one or two balloons, a sweater and a removable wall. Each object shows negative and positive charges. Charges are shown in pairs. Below the Play Area, a Control Panel allows you to add and remove objects, adjust charge views and reset the entire experiment. Currently, the sweater and wall have many pairs of charges, the balloon, just a few. Tab to navigate between objects. Select question mark for keyboard help.

Play Area

Place to experiment with balloons, sweater and wall.

Sweater

The Sweater has no more positive charges than negative ones. It is on the left side of the Play Area.

Balloon One

The balloon has no more negative charges than positive ones. Currently, positioned at equal distance between sweater and wall. The sweater is to the left and the wall is to the right. Select any Arrow key to grab and move balloon. Select Tab for next item. Select question mark for keyboard help.

Wall

The wall has no more positive charges than negative ones. It is located on the right side of the Play Area.

Control Panel

Toggle to conduct experiments with or without the wall.

Charge Settings

Select how you want to see or hear the charges.

Balloon Settings

Experiment with one or two balloons.

Returns balloon to initial position and an uncharged state.

Select to return entire experiment to original state.
