Keyboard Help

Balloon Interactions

  • Arrow Keys: Grabs & moves balloon in any direction.
  • Spacebar or Control plus Enter: Releases balloon.
  • Shift plus Arrow Key: Grabs & Moves balloon with bigger steps.

Quick Move Hotkey Combinations

  • J plus S: Jumps to edge of Sweater.
  • J plus W: Jumps to Wall.
  • J plus N: Jumps to Near Wall.
  • J plus M: Jumps to Middle of Play Area.

Sim Navigation & Help

  • Tab key: Goes to next focusable item.
  • Question Mark: Opens keyboard help.
